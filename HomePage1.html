Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and plans

You can check your balance and data usage by dialing *121# from your Mobi-Comm number. Alternatively, download our Mobi-Comm App for real-time tracking of all your usage details including voice, data, and SMS.

International roaming can be activated through the Mobi-Comm App or by calling our customer care at 1800-123-4567. We recommend activating it at least 24 hours before your international travel.

You'll need a valid ID proof (Aadhar, Passport, Driving License, or Voter ID) and address proof. Our digital KYC process makes verification simple, and you can upload these documents directly through our app or website.

The porting process typically takes 24-48 hours after we receive your port request and all required documents. Our team will guide you through the entire process to ensure a smooth transition.

Unused data expires with your current plan validity. However, our Weekend Data Rollover feature allows unused weekday data to be automatically carried forward to the weekend for all plans above ₹249.

All entertainment services including Mobi-Comm TV, Music Streaming and Movie Access can be enjoyed through the Mobi-Comm App. Simply download the app, log in with your Mobi-Comm number, and access the Entertainment section.
